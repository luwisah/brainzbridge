Instructions

Functionality

This template is made to be used as a starter for a NFT collection drop or other digital NFT artwork.

This template does not allow you to sell NFTs in Webflow, but instead links to your selected marketplace to sell there. One of the most popular NFT marketplaces right now is OpenSea.

‍

Adding NFT images

You can add NFT images to the website through the CMS. You might not need to add every individual NFT in your collection, but I would recommend adding at-least 30 to get full functionality throughout the site.


Customizable to suit your brand

Feel like changing something in the template? All of our templates where built using Webflow without writing code. That means you can customize them in every way to suit your brand. Learn more about how to customize Webflow sites at Help Center
